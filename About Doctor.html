Dr Pradnya Kanase

Apex Eye Care Clinic is owned and managed by Dr. Pradnya Dhairyasheel Kanase.

Dr. Pradnya Kanase completed her MBBS from Krishna Institute of Medical Sciences, Karad in 2002. Thereafter, she interned at Sasoon General Hospital, Pune for one year in 2003 and worked as Medical Officer at KEM Hospital, Pune for one year in 2004.

She completed her Post Graduation (D. OMS) from KEM Hospital, Pune in 2006 and FCPS from Lokmanya Tilak Municipal Medical College & General Hospital, Mumbai in 2007.

After completing her formal medical education, Dr. Pradnya Kanase worked as Consulting Ophthalmologist and Phaco Surgeon at NAB Eye Hospital, Chiplun for a period of one and a half years from 2007 to 2009.

Dr. Pradnya Kanase is also attached to Jupiter Hospital, Pune

About Us

Apex Eye Care focuses mainly on the treatment and care of patients with a wide range of eye problems from common problems like diminision of vision to rare conditions which need treatments.

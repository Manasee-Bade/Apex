Dr Pradnya Kanase

Apex eye care clinic is managed by Dr Pradnya Dhairyasheel Kanase

Dr. Pradnya Kanase completed her MBBS in 2002 from Krishna Institute of Medical Sciences, Karad, Shivaji University, Kolhapur. Thereafter she has worked as an intern at Sasson General hospital, Pune for one year in 2003 and also worked as medical officer at KEM hospital Pune for one year in 2004. She completed her post-graduation (D.OMS) from KEM hospital Pune within 2 years and then joined LTML hospital Sion Mumbai for further degree FCPS for one more year.

In 2007, after completing FCPS, she joined NAB eye hospital at Chiplun Maharashtra to serve the society with her skills and knowledge and worked as consultant ophthalmologist and phaco surgeon for almost one and half year.

About Us

Apex Eye Care focuses mainly on the treatment and care of patients with a wide range of eye problems from common problems like diminision of vision to rare conditions which need treatments.
